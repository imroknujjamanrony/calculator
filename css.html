Traffic jam is a common problem in our country. A traffic jam means a long line of vehicles stuck in jam. It is a regular scene in big cities and towns. Now it has become a serious problem. It creates a deplorable condition in our regular life. There are many causes of traffic jams. Increasing the number of buses, trucks, autorickshaws, and rickshaws are the main causes of traffic jams. Overpopulation is also a cause. On the other hand, the traffic control system in our country is not developed. Drivers are also responsible for this problem. Their lack of knowledge about traffic rules, overtaking tendency and parking here and there are one more causes of traffic jams. It causes untold suffering to the passengers and the movement of the vehicles.
